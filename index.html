Hi there! I'm Lauren, your go-to for all things front-end software development, web design and user experience design. As a computer science student and a passionate developer, I spend my days (and more nights than I’d like to admit) immersed in code, creating magic on digital canvases. Work

Throughout my training I've had the opportunity to work on some fantastic projects, each presenting unique challenges and learning opportunities. Here are a few of my favorites:

ThrillJunkies:

An ongoing project that will be an exciting tool for thrill enthusiasts. Filter worldwide theme parks and attractions based on your preferences, and save your favorites. Still under development with React, JSX, CSS, Bootstrap, and RESTful APIs.

Art Portfolio Site:
A multi-page platform showcasing an artist's portfolio. It just so happens to belong to my talented daughter! Built with React, JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and REST.

Tic-Tac-Toe:
A classic game with a digital twist. This two-player Tic-Tac-Toe game is built with JavaScript, HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, and AJAX.

Movie Review Site:
A movie review website simulation that enables users to rate and review movies. Crafted with React, JSX, CSS, Bootstrap, and REST.

About

My journey has brought me to the entertainment capital of the world, Las Vegas, Nevada, where I'm currently pursuing my Bachelors in Computer Science from WGU. Earlier this year I dove head first into Front End Software Development by participating in a six month online Bootcamp at the College of Southern Nevada, and graduating with a 99.45% overall grade. This incredible experience has allowed me to put my strong problem solving skills to work creating responsive websites that are as functional as they are aesthetically pleasing.

So far I’ve worn many hats in my career - from Sales Leadership, to Account Executive, to Technical Recruiter - and now launching my career as a Software Developer. But the journey has just begun! I’m a lifelong learner, and I bring that curiosity to my work. I love challenging myself and solving complex problems. I've been praised for my ability to collaborate with cross-functional teams, adapt to change, and communicate effectively. This eclectic mix of experiences and an immense desire to learn and grow make me a dynamic addition to any tech team. In the realm of front-end development, I bring more than just coding skills. I bring a unique perspective, an insatiable curiosity, and just a pinch of sarcasm, making my work more than just a job. It's a passion, an adventure, a quest that I'm excited to embark upon each and every day.

While I’m presently looking to break into my first full-time development gig, I'm available for freelance, contract work, and internships. I’m also always open to connecting with like-minded professionals and innovators, so let's build, grow, and pioneer together!
